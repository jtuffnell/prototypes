The purpose of the form is to better inform the secondary school about the pupil and best support the transition process. The use of one common form for all pupils moving from primary to secondary will also seek to reduce the burden on year six teachers having to complete many different forms for different schools. This form does not duplicate information already contained on the common transfer form (CTF) but it allows the opportunity to add detail if needed.

A form should be completed for each pupil moving to a Liverpool secondary school. No form needs to be completed for those pupils moving out-of-boundary.

When completing this form, please take into consideration that this form could be seen by the child’s parents or carers if requested. If you have specific concerns about the child you can always tick the final box at the end of the form; this will prompt the child’s secondary school to contact you directly. This can also be used if you wish to elaborate on any section of the form.

Questions that require an answer are marked with a red asterisk. To submit the form click the "Submit" button at the bottom. Once submitted you'll find a "submit another response" link to begin a new form for another pupil. These forms should be completed as soon as possible, at the latest by FRIDAY 14th MAY. We will collate the submissions, match to pupil admissions and contextual data and supply the results to the secondary schools.

EHAT – In this section, the teacher would need to name the lead professional and give contact details. Providing the EHAT number would be useful. If CAMHS are involved, the type of support and named worker with contact details is needed. Similarly for social care involvement where the social worker and his/her details should be given.

Information about EAL and medical concerns are given through the CTF, so no sections are given to this area. However, if there are issues that the secondary school needs to be alerted to, as highlighted above, in the final section there is a box that can be ticked indicating that the secondary school needs to contact the primary school for further information.

The transition process for SEND is dealt with separately so not included on this form.

Please note, all safeguarding information must be shared with the secondary school and should be securely delivered to them before the start of the autumn term.
